WHAT IS ILO?

The International Logic Olympiad

The International Logic Olympiad (ILO) is an international competition that celebrates logical thinking, critical analysis, and structured reasoning. It provides students worldwide with an opportunity to challenge their intellect through logic puzzles, pattern recognition, argument construction, and abstract reasoning tasks.

  • Global Participation
    Students from schools and academies around the world compete, representing their institutions and countries.
  • Focus on Reasoning
    Unlike typical academic contests, ILO emphasizes clear logical structure, deductive thinking, and argument precision.
  • Recognized Achievement
    Top performers earn global recognition and may qualify for advanced study programs or academic awards.
Saint Paul Preparatory Seoul

ILO Information & Practice Series

Hosted by Saint Paul Preparatory Seoul, this event helps students understand the International Logic Olympiad (ILO) and prepare through hands-on practice. Across short sessions, students learn core logic concepts, try puzzle drills, and take a mock test to get ready for the official ILO.

  • Information Sessions
    What ILO is, how it works, sample problem types, and tips for getting started.
  • Practice Workshops
    Basics of logic & puzzles, strategy building, and timed drills to improve accuracy and speed.
  • Mock-Test (Optional)
    Simulated round with feedback—use it as a checkpoint before the official ILO.
Open to Grades 6–10 (SPP Seoul & invited schools)
